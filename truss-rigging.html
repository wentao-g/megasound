Course - Truss Rigging

Master the skills of safe and professional truss rigging for live events.

Learn the fundamentals of truss rigging, from understanding load calculations and hardware selection to assembling, securing, and operating truss systems for concerts, theatre, exhibitions, and corporate events. Perfect for beginners and aspiring riggers looking to build a strong foundation in event rigging safety and professional setup.

Mastering the Art of Sound Mixing & Live Audio

This hands-on course is designed for aspiring and professional audio engineers who want to sharpen their skills in digital sound mixing using the Midas M32 Live Console. From console operation and signal routing to mixing techniques and live event audio management, you will gain practical experience in delivering high-quality sound for concerts, theatre, and corporate events.
