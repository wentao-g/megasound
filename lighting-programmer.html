Lighting Programmer

Mastering the Art of Stage & Event Lighting

This intensive course is designed for aspiring and working lighting professionals who want to sharpen their programming skills using the Avolites Quartz Lighting Board. From console operation and fixture setup to creating advanced show files, you will gain hands-on experience in lighting programming and designing stunning lighting looks for concerts, theatre, and live events.

Duration: 2 Days Price: $1200
